Full Journey / General Practice Recommended
Not sure where to start? This covers everything — mixed questions across all types and rounds, building your confidence from the ground up. Great for general practice or if you want the full interview experience.
👋
1st Round — HR Screen
Friendly HR interviewer. Culture fit, motivations, salary expectations, basic background check. Warm but professional tone.
💼
2nd Round — Hiring Manager
Direct and experienced. Deep dive into your work history, competencies and how you'd perform in the role. Expects specifics.
🔬
3rd Round — Technical / Case
Tough and detailed. Technical problem solving, case studies, and role-specific challenges. No fluff — prove your expertise.
🏆
Final Round — Executive / Panel
Senior stakeholders. Strategic thinking, leadership, vision and cultural alignment. High stakes, high expectations.
