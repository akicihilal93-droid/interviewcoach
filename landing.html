Does it work for school admissions, not just jobs?
+
Yes — we have a dedicated school admissions track covering undergraduate, graduate, law, medical and MBA interviews. The questions, feedback and scoring are all calibrated for academic panels, not corporate recruiters.
How honest is the feedback really?
+
Uncompromisingly honest. If your answer was weak, vague, or off-topic — it says so directly. Words like "great", "wonderful", "insightful" are explicitly banned. You get clinical, factual scoring only.
Do I need to upload my resume?
+
No — it's optional. If you upload your CV and job description, the questions are tailored to your specific background. Without it, you get well-rounded general interview practice.
What devices does it work on?
+
Any device with a browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop. Works best in Chrome or Edge. No app download required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Yes, cancel anytime with no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
+
AcedIt is purpose-built for interview practice. It has a real voice interviewer, structured feedback scoring, speech analysis, ATS rating, example answers, and a full report system. ChatGPT is a general chatbot — this is a dedicated coaching platform.
